How it works (÷9):
10 = 1×9 + 1 → leaves digit
100 = 11×9 + 1 → leaves digit
1000 = 111×9 + 1 → leaves digit
Each place value is "multiple of 9 + 1". Multiply by digit d: the 9-part vanishes, only d remains. Total remainder = digit sum mod 9.
