Why does "add the digits to check divisibility by 9" work? And why does the alternating digit sum check 11? It's all about what each place value leaves behind when divided. Watch the dots get grouped into 9s (or 11s) for each digit, and the leftovers — the digits themselves — are exactly what determines the remainder.
